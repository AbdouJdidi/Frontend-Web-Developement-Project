Indonesia

Bali - Island of Gods

Known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich culture, Bali is a popular destination for surfers, spiritual seekers, and those looking for a tropical paradise.

Komodo National Park:

Home to the famous Komodo dragons, this UNESCO World Heritage site offers a unique opportunity to see these giant lizards in their natural habitat.

Borobudur and Prambanan Temples:

Located in Yogyakarta, these ancient temples are UNESCO World Heritage sites and represent the pinnacle of Indonesian Buddhist and Hindu art and architecture.

Bali - Cultural and Beach Experience

Explore Ubud, known for its art and cultural heritage. Visit the Monkey Forest and Ubud Palace.

Visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces and explore local art markets.

Attend a traditional Balinese dance performance and enjoy a beachside dinner in Seminyak.

Yogyakarta - Cultural and Historical Exploration

Fly to Yogyakarta and visit Borobudur, the world's largest Buddhist temple.

Explore Prambanan, a Hindu temple complex, and the nearby Ratu Boko archaeological site.

Wander around Malioboro Street for shopping and local cuisine.

Fly to Sorong and take a boat to Raja Ampat, a paradise for scuba diving and snorkeling.
